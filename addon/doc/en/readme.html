What's New

Experience the new Old Log functionality—an intelligent way to recover and review your logs from previous sessions instantly, ensuring no critical data is lost after a system restart.

Features

logViewer transforms the standard NVDA Log Viewer into a high-performance diagnostic tool. Designed for developers and power users, it brings professional-grade editing features directly into your workflow:

  • Advanced Search Engine:
    Harness the power of Control+F. Our search dialog supports Case-sensitivity, Wrap-around navigation, and Regular Expressions. Plus, it remembers your search history for rapid re-use.
  • Instant Navigation:
    Move through results at lightning speed. Use F3 for the next match or Shift+F3 for the previous one—all without ever re-opening a dialog.
  • Precision Bookmarking:
    Never lose your place again. Tag critical events with Control+F2 and navigate between your custom markers effortlessly using F2 and Shift+F2.

Old Log Functionality

Stop worrying about losing logs after a crash. Our refined recovery system ensures your data is always within reach.

Reliable Log Recovery

  • Direct Session Access: Instantly retrieve the nvda-old.log from your system's temporary directory. No manual searching required.
  • One-Key Activation: Press NVDA+Control+L to immediately open your previous session's log in your preferred external text editor.
  • Optimized for Debugging: By targeting the system's generated old log, you get the most accurate snapshot of what happened right before a crash or restart.

Resilience Against Crashes

When NVDA crashes and restarts, the history of the failed session is preserved by the system. logViewer acts as your dedicated gateway to this data, allowing you to bypass technical hurdles and jump straight into analysis and troubleshooting.

Universal Language Support

Built for the global NVDA community, logViewer works seamlessly in any language.

By utilizing advanced Window Handle (HWND) detection instead of relying on localized window titles, we guarantee 100% compatibility across all language versions of NVDA.
